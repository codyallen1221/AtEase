---
## Components

### Buttons
- Rectangular with soft corners (6–8px)
- Primary: solid Deep Olive
- Secondary: outline Charcoal
- No pill buttons
- No gradients

### Cards
- Light background
- 6–8px radius
- Subtle border OR soft shadow (never both)

### Inputs
- White background
- 1px border
- Focus state uses border color only (no glow)
---

## Icons

- Stroke-based icons only
- Consistent size across app
- Icons must serve a purpose

---

## Motion

- 150–200ms transitions
- Ease-in-out only
- No bounce or playful motion
- Motion should explain, not impress

---

## Copy & Tone

- Plain language
- Short sentences
- No marketing jargon
- Button text describes the action (“Save changes”)

---

## Design Filter

Before shipping any screen, ask:

> “Would this feel calm and trustworthy at 10pm when the user is tired?”

If no, simplify.
